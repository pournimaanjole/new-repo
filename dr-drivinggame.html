HOW TO PLAY-DR DRIVING GAME:

Troubleshooting Common Problems

If you’re experiencing problems with Dr Driving, here are some troubleshooting tips:

  1. Make sure you have the latest version of the game installed.
  2. Clear the game’s cache and data.
  3. Restart your device.
  4. Make sure you have a stable internet connection.

Tips for Winning Races

To win races in Dr Driving, you need to be both fast and careful. Here are some tips to help you improve your driving skills:

  • Follow the traffic rules and drive safely.
  • Avoid crashing into other cars, buildings, or obstacles.
  • Use the handbrake to perform tight turns or drifts.
  • Watch out for pedestrians and other vehicles on the road.
  • Upgrade your car to improve its speed and handling.
  • Complete missions quickly to earn more coins and gold.
